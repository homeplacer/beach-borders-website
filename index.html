Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does concrete curbing last?

Done right, decades. Because we mix the color all the way through, reinforce with fiber and seal twice, our curbing resists fading, chipping and cracking far longer than sprayed-on alternatives. A light re-seal every few years keeps it looking new.

Will it crack or move over time?

Our curbing is fiber-reinforced and installed with proper base prep and control joints to handle Carolina's freeze-thaw and clay soil. We also build in drainage so water moves away instead of pooling under the curb.

Can you match my home or HOA's colors?

Almost always. We offer a wide range of integral colors and stamp patterns and bring samples to your free estimate. If you have an HOA requirement, bring it — we'll find a match.

How much does it cost?

Curbing is usually priced per linear foot, and the total depends on length, profile, color and stamp. The honest answer is we'll measure and give you a free, no-pressure estimate — no guessing games.

How long does the job take?

Most residential projects are completed in just one to two days, start to finish, including pouring, shaping, coloring and sealing.

Do you do more than just curbing?

Yes. We also handle walkways, pads, paver patios, mulch, rock, sod, drainage and tree & shrub installation — one local crew for the whole yard.
